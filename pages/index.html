And your countdown starts ... now!

Welcome to Countdown, a popular game show from Channel 4 bought to the internet! Either on your own or with another player, the aim is to make the largest word from a choice of nine letters in 30 seconds. The longest and valid word will win points. The player with the most points wins the game.

To start, choose either of the options. Multiplayer games will be matchmade automatically, your opponent will be random!
